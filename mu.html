Mothers Union (MU)

The Mothers Union is a fellowship of Christian women dedicated to prayer, service, and supporting families in the church and community. Our mission is to strengthen family life and nurture faith among women.

Our Vision

To empower women to be pillars of faith, love, and service in their families, church, and society.

Activities

- Prayer meetings and Bible study
- Family support and counseling
- Community outreach and charity
- Women empowerment seminars
- Church events and hospitality

Join Us

All women are welcome! For more information, contact the parish office or join us during our next meeting.
